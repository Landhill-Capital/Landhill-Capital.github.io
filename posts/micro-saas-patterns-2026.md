Micro-SaaS teams continue to win by solving narrow, painful problems faster than broad platforms.

## What stands out

- Teams prioritize retention before growth.
- Product onboarding is becoming more opinionated.
- Distribution often starts from a single channel that compounds.

## Why it matters

Focused execution creates stronger feedback loops and better capital efficiency.
